ℹ️ What is MP3IFY?

.txtaud is an innovative text-based audio format that encodes MP3 data into a human-readable JSON structure. The Arduino Music feature converts melodies into Arduino sketches for MKR IoT Carrier with LEDs and buzzer support! Upload MP3/txtaud files for automatic conversion.
