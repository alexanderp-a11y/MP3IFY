ℹ️ What is MP3IFY?

.txtaud is an innovative text-based audio format that encodes MP3 data into a human-readable JSON structure. With MP3IFY, you can convert MP3 files to .txtaud, play them back, and even generate Arduino sketches to play your music on microcontroller boards with LED effects!
